CRICKET

Cricket is a bat-and-ball sport with two teams.
One team bats to score runs, while the other fields and tries to get them out. The game is played on an oval field with a 22-yard-long pitch.

SOCCER

Soccer, or football, is the world's most popular sport. It involves two teams of eleven players each, aiming to score goals by getting a ball into the opposing team's net using any part of the body except the hands and arms.

BASE-BALL

Baseball is a team sport played with a ball and bat. One team hits while the other fields.
The goal is to score runs by hitting and running around bases.

Golf

Playing the ball from a teeing ground into a hole by successive strokes in accordance with the rules.
A cross-country game in which a player strikes a small ball with various clubs from a series of starting points into a series of holes on a course.
